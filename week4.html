Rotation 4: Laser Cutting, Engraving and Woodshop

In this rotation, we learnt how to operate the laser cutting machine to laser cut different objects from wood and cardboard

First, I had to design and make a clock. For the design, I used CorelDraw. I imported different images from the interenet, traced their bitmaps and then combined them to obtain the final design.Then, I used the EpilogLaser Fusion machine to cut it. I first had to test cut on a cardboard to make sure that the the clock comes out satisfactorily, and then I cut my final product on wood.

Secondly, I had to design and make a fit kit. I used corel draw to design a circle, a square and a hexagon. To make them combinable, I created small rectangle joints, set them to hair line
